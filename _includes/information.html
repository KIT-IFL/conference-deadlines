Features of Deadlines.info
Countdown view (click here)
  • To view deadlines in more detail, click on the icon . Deadline in different timezones and calendar downloads are available.
  • You can directly access the conference website by clicking the icon .
  • You can download the deadline to import it into your calendar.
  • You can filter by using the dropdown menus or by clicking on the badges below each conference deadline.
Calendar view (click here)
  • You can click on a date to show the detailed conference pages. Note, that currently this only works if only one event takes place on that date.
General
  • On each conference specific website, you can download the conference deadline and dates for your calendar.
  • You can optionally export all deadlines to Google Calendar or .ics.
  • Deadlines are shown in America/New_York time.
  • To add/edit a conference, send in a pull request.
